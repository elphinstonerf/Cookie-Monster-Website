About Me

From there, I fell into a rabbit hole and found a passion in software development. I started researching and practicing with online tutorials, which increased my desire to advance my education. Now, using credits from my previous associate degree, I will be proudly receiving my bachelor's degree from Southern New Hampshire University in November 2022 with a concentration in coding.
          I have thoroughly enjoyed starting to use my skills in development of projects such as the python text adventure game Spooky Street, as well as the C++ dice game simulation of Left Center Right. My family has also encouraged me in fun ways. My daughter has asked me to create a website to host her gallery of her future famous artwork. I consider myself lucky to find a career that I am consistently excited to learn more about, and look forward to creating more.
